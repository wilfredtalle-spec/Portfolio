About Me

I am Wilfred Djoko Talle, a mechanical and robotic design engineer focused on creating systems that support global economic stability and uplift underserved communities. With a background in Mechanical Engineering Technology and graduate studies in Intelligent Robotic Systems, I combine mechanical design, automation, and intelligent control to build practical, impactful solutions.

My goal is to grow as either a Mechanical Design Engineer or a Robotic Automation Engineer, developing technologies that reduce costs, increase efficiency, and open new opportunities for people at every social level. I believe engineering should empower society — especially those in the lowest economic classes — by improving access to essential resources and enabling sustainable income growth.
